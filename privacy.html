Privacy Policy

We believe in minimal data collection. Here's exactly what happens with your information when you use CamperBypass.

Effective: July 2, 2026
4 min read
Your Data, Protected

01 Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how CamperBypass ("the Bot," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you interact with our Discord bot. We are committed to minimizing data collection and protecting your privacy.

Our philosophy: We collect the absolute minimum data necessary. CamperBypass is designed to be privacy-first — your links are not logged, stored, or shared.

02 Data We Collect

2.1 — Automatically Collected Data

When you use the Bot, the following data may be temporarily processed in memory to fulfill your request:

  • Discord User ID — Used to identify who is issuing a command
  • Server (Guild) ID — Used to identify the server context
  • Channel ID — Used to know where to send the response
  • Submitted URL — Processed in real-time and not persisted

2.2 — What We Do NOT Collect

We want to be explicitly clear: CamperBypass does not collect, log, or store:

  • Your submitted URLs (processed in-memory only, never written to disk or database)
  • The resolved/destination URLs returned to you
  • Message content beyond the command and URL
  • IP addresses
  • Personal information such as names, emails, or phone numbers
  • Browser or device information
  • Any form of tracking cookies or pixels

03 How We Use Data

The minimal data we process is used solely for:

  • Command execution — Processing your link bypass request and returning the result
  • Response delivery — Sending the resolved URL back to the correct channel
  • Rate limiting — Temporarily tracking command frequency in-memory to prevent abuse
  • Error handling — Identifying and reporting errors that occur during processing

We do not use your data for analytics, advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond Bot functionality.

04 Data Storage & Retention

CamperBypass is designed with a zero-retention architecture:

  • Submitted URLs and resolved links are processed in-memory and immediately discarded after the response is sent
  • No URLs are written to any database, log file, or external service
  • Rate-limit counters are held in-memory and reset automatically
  • We do not maintain user profiles, histories, or databases of any kind
Technical detail: The Bot's link processing pipeline does not include any persistence layer. Data flows in, gets processed, flows out, and is garbage-collected — nothing is saved.

05 Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your data with any third parties. Period.

The only scenario where data may leave the Bot's immediate processing environment is when the submitted URL itself is followed/resolved — this is inherent to how link bypassing works, and the destination server may log the request according to their own privacy policy. We have no control over third-party server logging.

06 Discord's Data Practices

Discord, as a platform, collects and processes data independently of CamperBypass. By using Discord, you are subject to Discord's Privacy Policy. Our Bot operates within Discord's infrastructure and has access only to the data that Discord's API provides — nothing more.

07 Security

We implement reasonable measures to protect the Bot's infrastructure, including:

  • Rate limiting to prevent abuse and denial-of-service attacks
  • Input validation and sanitization on all user-provided URLs
  • Regular updates to dependencies to patch known vulnerabilities
  • Access controls on the Bot's hosting environment

However, no system is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, but we are committed to maintaining a safe tool.

08 Children's Privacy

CamperBypass does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since we do not collect personal information at all, this is inherently satisfied. If you believe a child has interacted with the Bot inappropriately, please contact us.

09 Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights regarding your data. Since CamperBypass does not store your data, most data rights requests are inherently satisfied. However, you may:

  • Request data deletion — We have nothing to delete, but we'll confirm this upon request
  • Request data access — We can confirm what data points are temporarily processed
  • Opt out — Simply remove the Bot from your server
  • Lodge a complaint — Contact us or your local data protection authority

10 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this page periodically. If we make material changes, we may announce them through our Discord support server.

11 Contact

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please reach out through our Discord support server.
